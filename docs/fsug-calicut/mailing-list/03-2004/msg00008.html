No official proceedings, affiliations to form an FSUG or GNU/Linux
user group. First you need a website. for examples look at

http://www.geocities.com/fsug_calicut
http://puggy.symonds.net/~fsug-kochi
http://glugt.linuxisle.com
http://www.gnu.org
http://www.gnu.org.in

and a mailing list. and then, ofcourse, members... If there is a regular
place for monthly meetings it'll be an added advantage. You can make
use of posters to get your friends know what is free software and
GNU/Linux. 

Some examples of posters and other materials are available from

http://savannah.nongnu.org/projects/demo-schools
http://puggy.symonds.net/~fsug-kochi/downloads/press_kit.tar.gz
http://glugt.linuxisle.com/poster.pdf

Best of luck
