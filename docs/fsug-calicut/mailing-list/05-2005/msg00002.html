Dear Friends,

     At Kannur, in recent meeting of the teachers association it was
discussed to introduce and  use free software/GNU/Linux based
applications for IT education in maximum number of schools. A major
section of the teachers are now aware of the importance of the free
software in schools and they wanted to get trained on GNU/Linux
system. It  would be nice if they get the Govt. Supported training on
GNU/Linux, but it is not yet clear how soon it will be implemented.
Nevertheless, teachers are organising their own meetings and trying to
get training on GNU/Linux systems.
 
Kannur District has large number of schools covering wide area  among
rural population. To support schools at different localities, it was
decided to make a core group of dedicated members  of free software
GNU/Linux community
available from Kannur/nearby  district to help the teachers in getting
them trained on setting up GNU/Linux system in their schools.
  
We require to form team  members from different locality willing to
support the schools.
 If anyone can help in this regard please contact me. 

  Next meeting of teachers association is scheduled on May 16 at KSTA
office,Kannur. If we can  convince them that there is enough support
available locally, then that will be a great motivation for them to
implement new setup soon after summer vacation. This is a crucial
time, and we have to  support them.
   
 A detailed work plan has to be brought out in the next meeting.
