Hello friends,
    
  PHOENIX, the innovative laboratory instrument kit for
science/engineering college is now available on order basis.
PHOENIX is the new way of doing experiment in teaching laboratory. It
was designed & developed  by NSC, an inter-university research lab of
UGC. (see http://www.nsc.res.in/~elab/phoenix/).  It is an ideal tool
for student projects. Engineering students can learn the
circuitry,interfacing technique, and write their own programs.

  The design,circuit,softwares all are open. 

   The features includes, 

 * 8 channels of  ADC( Analog to Digital Converter)
 * 4 channels of Programmable DAC
 * Programmable Digital Input/Outputs
 * Wave form Signal Generator
 * constant current source
 * Stepper motor Controller
 * Relay Drives
 * Device Driver& Software
 * GNU/Linux bootable Live CD
 *
 For Details on the project please visit  http://www.nsc.res.in/~elab/phoenix/
An article on PHOENIX see http://linuxgazette.net/111/pramode.html
