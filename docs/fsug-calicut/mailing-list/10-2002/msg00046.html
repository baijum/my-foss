Debian does  not follow the  `version number madness', they  release a
stable version  only after  an extensive period  of testing  & freeze.

it has  the most simple  installer.  Debian unstable `sid'  will never
get released,  the packages in this  change on a  daily basis.  Debian
testing `sarge' will eventually get released after about an year or so
as stable `sarge'.

I use `sid' on my Desktop machine and have deployed `woody' across all
servers.  Best of both worlds...ehh.
