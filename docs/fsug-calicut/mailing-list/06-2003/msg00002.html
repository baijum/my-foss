News Report from Kannur,

News Clipping from Mathrubhoomi, Malayala Manorama & Other Malayalam Dailies, appeared on June 8th, Kannur Edition.

-----
 "GNU/Linux in school computers in Kannur"

Using MPLAD( Member of Parliament Local Area Development) Fund, Computer education facility has been setup in 54 Govt High schools in Kannur and Wynad district of Kerala. All these computer facility will be completely using GNU/Linux and other free software. A one day
workshop for the teachers of these schools have been conducted at Chamber of Commerce hall at Kannur and it was inaugurated by MP Shri A P Abdullakutty. The function was presided by deputy director of education Shri K Ravindran. Altogether 119 teachers from 54 schools attended the workshop.
