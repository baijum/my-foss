Hi to all,

There will be a One Day Orientation programme for school teachers

on GNU/Linux system at Kannur on June 7th. The programme is being arranged by district planning board under the MPLAD( Member of Parliament Local Area Development) programme of Kannur M P Shri A P Abdullakutty. Under MPLAD 43 govt schools in Kannur have got computer education facility setup based on LTSP network on GNU/Linux operating system.

Around 130 teachers from these schools are invited to participate in the programme.

In addition members from planning board, NIC, directorate of education will also be participating in this programme. The opening session will be inaugurated by Sri A P Abdullakutty at 9.30 AM. The training session will be from 10 to 5 PM and is being conducted by

Swathantra Software Solutions & Support ( S2S2), Munderi Kannur.


Venue: Chamber of Commerce Hall, Kannur
Date: 7 -June 2003
Duration 9.30- 5 PM
