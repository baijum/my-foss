Dear friends,
      Kerala Social Forum  happening at Thrissur in this December. It is the 
State level event before World Social forum. Free Software Foundation is 
decided to participate in World Social Forum 2004 held at mumbai.
http://gnu.org.in/fsfiwsf.html 

according to the charter of principles social forums are 

" ... an open meeting place for reflective thinking, democratic debate of 
ideas, formulation of proposals, free exchange of experiences and inter-linking 
for effective action, by groups and movements of civil society that are opposed 
to neo- liberalism and to domination of the world by capital and any form of 
imperialism, and are committed to building a society centred on the human 
person".
http://wsfindia.org/charter.php

    stallman is visited in asian social forum held at hyderabaad. 

the kerala Social forum process is facing the problem of  hijacking from the 
left parties. but as being who is egar about the freedom of the each and every 
activities from a particular group or organization  can?t avoid the situation 
which is need for action . the present condition is that the  KSSP ,whom they 
are working for left are ready to share the space in the democratic manner . 
but we should be vigil about our space . if we are not using our space it will 
go to their account . so we should participate actively  to protect our space 
for pluralism and democratic processes of  the noble concept of  swathantra 

   I think we must participate in  the kerala social forum.We can conduct  
seminars, work shops, open forums and exhibitions there.they will provide the 
space. This is a good opportunity to an active participation and widen our Free 
Software politics to the common people 

for more information about KSF and programme registration contact at their mail 
address: ksf2003@xxxxxxxxxx

for informations about socialforum process visit
http://www.wsfindia.org/
