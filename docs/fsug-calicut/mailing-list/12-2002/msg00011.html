Hello friends,

   Today (12/12/2002) a press conference, conducted by
fsug calicut, was held at Calicut press club. Mr.
Ajith Kumar Sr.Scientist (New Delhi) , Dr. Sreee Kumar
were present. They talked about the impact of free
software that could be made in the projects like
IT@School and e-governance.

   Tomorrow (13/12/2002 , 5.00 PM) , fsug calicut
conducts a meeting at Calicut press club (Near
Muthalakulam) on the occation of first anniversary of
the FSUG-Calicut for making public awareness of
free-software in the light of current global scenario.
This meeting will be inaugurated by Mrs.Sheela V.S Sr.
System Designer (CEDTI,Calicut). Mr. Ajith Kumar will
conduct a demo on how to utilise free softwares in
various projects like IT@School, e-governance and
other areas where security is a very important issue.
You come to this meeting with your friends.
