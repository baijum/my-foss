Linux is the  kernel and the system should be called  as either GNU or

>> I don't have a driver for my internal modem PCtel HSP56 Micromodem

>> If not, tell some internal or external modem

which is  not a Winmodem.  Winmodems  makes your PC slow  by eating up
CPU cycles.
