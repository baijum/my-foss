About mailing list

The purpose of this mailing list is to discuss various topics related to FSUG-Calicut, technical and non-techincal issues can discuss here.

How to subscribe

To recieve messages and to be able to post, you are required to subscribe. Send an email message to:

fsug-calicut-request@freelists.org

In the subject, enter this, and only this:

subscribe

You do not have to put anything in the body, anything put in the body of the email will be ignored. You Will then be sent a confirmation email, just reply to that message without any changes to confirm your subscription.

How to send an email to the list

To post a message to the list for everyone to read, send it to the following email address:

fsug-calicut@freelists.org

That's all you need to do! When you send it, it will take a few minutes to reach everyone, including you.

How to unsubscribe

If you no longer wish to recieve messages from FSUG-Calicut's mailing list, send an email message to:

fsug-calicut-request@freelists.org

In the subject, enter this, and only this:

unsubscribe

You do not have to put anything in the body, any thing put in the body of the email will be ignored.
