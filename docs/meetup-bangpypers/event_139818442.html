EMC Defenders League 2013

  • September 23, 2013 · 9:00 AM

With an ongoing mission to reinforce trust in IT, EMC introduces a contest that tests and rewards skills and knowledge on IT Security. We bring you EMC Defenders League, the Ultimate Security Challenge. Test your skills through a multitude of challenges and enhance your knowledge on IT security.

The contest kicks off with the preliminary Defenders Online Challenge comprising three levels spread over three weeks. The top 30 qualifiers will then fight for the ultimate glory by striking and defending a common objective in the grand finale – Defenders Arena Face-Off.

Gear up for the Ultimate Security Challenge. Reinforce Trust in IT. Join the EMC Defenders League today.

For more details please visit www.emcdefendersleague2013.com
